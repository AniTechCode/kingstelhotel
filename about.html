About Us

Discover the legacy of luxury and excellence that defines Kingstel Hotel.

Welcome to Kingstel Hotel

Kingstel hotel is a three star hotel located in a very plush area of Sekondi-Takoradi, oil city of Ghana perfected for the business and leisure-traveler with 60 ultra-modern rooms made up Standard, Deluxe, Superior Deluxe and Executive Suite to choose..

All our rooms have balconies, queen size beds, air conditions, refrigerator, mini bar, free high-speed Wi-Fi and wired internet, 32 inch LED TV’s with more than 11 channels, hand dryer, safe box, telephone and a comfortable office desk. Our Executive Suite rooms have been designed exclusively for your long stay where you will have total privacy. Fully furnished living area, 32 LED TV with more than 11 channels, kitchenette, and ultra-modern bathroom come with both a Jacuzzi and shower..
